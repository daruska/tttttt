Our team

It all started in October 2017. This was the year when intermediary was created – a creative open space for those who are passionate about their job and in pursue of constant improvement, sharing the same values and respect personal and cultural diversity.

The headquarter was established in Lviv, Ukraine, while the main development center was located in Lviv; one of the largest cities in Ukraine and the most famous IT hub across the country. Involved mostly in outsourcing projects, our clients were first from Europe and later from the USA and Canada.

From the very beginning, we have aimed at creating a corporate culture that would eliminate, where possible, all the barriers created by traditional organization and have embraced the organic principles with a high degree of self-management. Trust is the foundation of our organization. We cultivate a warm and close relationship with clients and among colleagues.

We have implemented a unique employee selection process “Colleagues choose colleagues”, which helped us to to create an environment where everybody enjoys working together and to attract highly motivated people who are capable of providing high quality software development services.
